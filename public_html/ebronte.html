Wuthering Heights: Fantasy and Realism
In this video from the British Library (4:29), Professor John Bowen considers Emily Brontë’s combination of fantasy and reality in Wuthering Heights and the way in which fairy tale and Gothic elements ‘haunt the edges’ of the novel. Video also available at YouTube, here.

Wuthering Heights: Landscape
In this video from the British Library (3:26), Professor John Bowen discusses the harshness of the landscape around Haworth and the central part it plays in the writings of the Brontë sisters. Filmed on location on the moors around Haworth. Video also available at YouTube, here.

Who is Heathcliff?
In this video from the British Library (3:46), Professor John Bowen discusses the enigmatic Heathcliff, from his early appearance as a rejected child, to his return as a powerful and violent antihero. Filmed on location on the moors around Haworth. Video also available at YouTube, here.
