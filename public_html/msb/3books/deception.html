Thematic Study

In a theme study students read books that were originally published in three different time periods but which have a common theme. They analyze commonalities and differences.

These themes have been approved for the Three Books Project. If you have an idea for another title or theme, please come and talk to me about it.

Theme: Deception

    • Puddn'head Wilson by Mark Twain
    • The Age of Innocence by Edith Wharton

    Middle

    • The Great Gatsby or Tender is the Night by F. Scott Fitzgerald
    • Invisible Man by Ralph Ellison
    • Native Son by Richard Wright
    • Elmer Gantry by Sinclair Lewis
    • All My Sons, The Crucible, and Death of a Salesman by Arthur Miller (three plays = one novel)

    Recent

    • A Gathering of Old Men by Ernest J. Gaines
    • All the King's Men by Robert Penn Warren
