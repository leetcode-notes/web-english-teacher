Genre: Humor

For a project on American humor, read one early, middle, and contemporary work. Ask yourself how the American sense of humor has developed (changed/stayed the same) over time. Choose from among these writers/titles or suggest one to me:

Early

  • Benjamin Franklin, essays and newspaper columns
  • Mark Twain, short stories

Middle

  • Robert Benchley
  • O. Henry
  • Ring Lardner
  • S. J. Perelman
  • Dorothy Parker
  • William Saroyan
  • Robert Sherwood
  • James Thurber

Contemporary

  • Dave Barry
  • Erma Bombeck
  • Bill Bryson (The Lost Continent or I'm a Stranger Here Myself)
  • Garrison Keillor
  • P. J. O'Rourke
  • David Sedaris
  • Neil Simon
  • Bailey White
