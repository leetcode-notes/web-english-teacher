Genre: Horror Fiction

Read one early, middle, and contemporary work. Ask yourself how the genre has developed (changed/stayed the same) over time. Choose from among these titles or suggest one to me:

Early

  • Edgar Allan Poe, short stories
  • Ambrose Bierce, short stories

Middle

  • H. P. Lovecraft, short stories
  • The Haunting of Hill House by Shirley Jackson
  • Johnny Got His Gun by Dalton Trumbo

Contemporary

  • Salem's Lot, The Green Mile, Dolores Claiborne, It, Pet Sematary, Stephen King
  • The Devil in the White City: Murder, Magic, and Madness at the Fair That Changed America, Erik Larson
    (Note: this work is nonfiction.)
  • The Silence of the Lambs: A Novel, Thomas Harris
  • The Bottoms, Joe R. Lansdale
  • Mr. X, In the Night Room, Lost Boy, Lost Girl, Magic Terror: Seven Tales, Peter Straub
