Achilles heel, Achilles tendon

That's right!

A person's Achilles heel is his or her weak point. For example, Cookie Monster's Achilles heel is cookies. He will do anything for a cookie!

The Achilles tendon joins the heel to the knee and goes up the back of the leg. An athlete who hurts an Achilles tendon may need surgery and will have a long recovery.
