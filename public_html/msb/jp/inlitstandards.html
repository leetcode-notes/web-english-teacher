This WebQuest addresses the following Information Literacy Standards:

  • The student accesses information efficiently and effectively.
  • The student evaluates information critically and competently.
  • The student uses information accurately and creatively.
  • The student pursues information related to personal interests.
  • The student appreciates literature and other creative expressions of information.
  • The student strives for excellence in information seeking and knowledge generation.
  • The student contributes positively to the learning community and recognizes the importance of information to a democratic society.
  • The student practices ethical behavior in regard to information and information technology.
  • The student participates effectively in groups to pursue and generate information.
