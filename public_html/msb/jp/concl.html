In Jurassic Park author Michael Crichton reminds readers that knowledge and judgement are not the same thing, and that gaining knowledge without also improving judgement can be very dangerous.

During this WebQuest you have developed your ability to judge the information on a Web site. You know that you must be alert for signs of bias and for inaccuracies.

Good judgement is important, whether you're researching something online, watching a TV commercial, or listening to a politician speak.

As essayist Michel Montaigne once wrote, "We can be knowledgeable with other men's knowledge, but we cannot be wise with other men's wisdom."
