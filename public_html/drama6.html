Drama Lesson Plans by Playwright, U-Z

Lesson plans and activities for plays are listed by the name of the playwright. Special collections are listed below by playwright. Theater resources in general are listed by the title of the Web site.

Oscar Wilde
Lesson plans for The Importance of Being Earnest, more.

Thornton Wilder
Biography, lesson plans and activities for teaching Our Town.

Tennessee Williams Teaching Resources
Suggestions for The Glass Menagerie and A Streetcar Named Desire.

August Wilson Teaching Resources
Lesson plans and activities for Fences, The Piano Lesson, Seven Guitars, Joe Turner's Come and Gone, Ma Rainey's Black Bottom Blues, and Two Trains Running.

The Country Wife
A study guide for the play by William Wycherly. Includes discussion of characterization, technique, costume, the set, scene-by-scene analysis, and possible essay questions.
