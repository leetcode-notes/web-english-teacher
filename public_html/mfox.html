Koala Lou

Koala Lou: Sequencing and Beginning, Middle, End
After reading the story Koala Lou aloud, students practice sequencing the events of the story and identifying the beginning, middle, and end. Upon completing this activity in cooperative groups they write using a teacher given prompt, including proper sequence and beginning, middle, and end. They also share the final product with their cooperative group. This lesson is designed for grade 3.
