The Clay Marble by Minfong Ho
Author biography, background information, and a wealth of support materials: prereading activities, vocabulary, graphic organizers, comprehension questions, and more. Access to this 22-page document requires Adobe Reader or compatible application.

The Clay Marble by Minfong Ho, Learn it Guide. Adobe Reader required for these resources.

  • Introductory Material
    Author biography and background information, character list, vocabulary words.
  • Preface-Chapter 6 Worksheet
    Interpreting a map, understanding homographs, making predictions.
  • Chapters 7-12 Worksheet
    Charting causes and effects, using context clues, making predictions.
  • Chapters 13-Afterword
    Visualizing scenes, finding synonyms, going beyond the novel.
  • Theme Graphic Organizer
    Students respond to questions in order to identify a theme.
  • First-Person Point of View Organizer
    Students explore changes in Dara by looking at what she thinks and says.
  • Motivation Graphic Organizer
    Students explore the feelings/ideas, experiences, and personal qualities that motivate characters in specific situations.
  • Whole-Novel Discussion and Activities
    5 discussion questions and 4 post-reading activities.
  • Multiple Choice Test
    10 questions.
