A Stillness at Appomatox by Bruce Catton. Adobe Reader required for these resources.

  • Introductory Material
    Author biography, background information, character list, vocabulary words.
  • Segment I Worksheet
    Students analyze characterization, work with context clues, and make predictions.
  • Segment II Worksheet
    Students analyze the conditions during the march and their effects on the troops. They create semantic maps and make predictions.
  • Segment III Worksheet
    Students identify causes and effects. They work with context clues and make predictions.
  • Segment IV Worksheet
    Students visualize and draw a scene. They work with vocabulary and think beyond the novel.
  • Allusion Graphic Organizer
    Students identify the meaning of specific allusions.
  • Imagery Graphic Organizer
    Given a list of images, students identify a symbolic meaning for each.
  • Theme Graphic Organizer
    Given a theme, students find events or behaviors that support it.
  • Whole-Novel Discussion and Activities
    5 discussion questions and 5 post-reading activities.
  • Multiple Choice Test
    10 questions.
