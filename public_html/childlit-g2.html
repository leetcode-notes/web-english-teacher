Children's Literature Lesson Plans, Authors Gillette-Greene

Dinosaur Ghosts: The Mystery of Coelophysis by J. Lynett Gillette
This writing task includes a prompt, graphic organizer, student handouts, and a model essay. Emphasis is on providing evidence to support an inference. 9 pages; word processor required for access.

The Gypsy Princess by Phoebe Gilman
Summary, analysis, and 4 sets of discussion questions.

Fred Gipson
Lesson plans for Old Yeller.

Bridge to America by Linda Glaser
This teacher's guide includes activities, discussion questions, and writing activities.

A Dollar for Penny by Julie Glass
Comprehension questions that focus on economic principles of price, goods and services, trade and money, and producers.

Mystic Horse by Paul Goble
Students note details as they read. Includes text-dependent questions, vocabulary words, a culminating writing task, and additional learning activities. Word processor required for access.

The Pot that Juan Built by Nancy Andrews-Goebel
Background, prereading questions, vocabulary, discussion questions, ideas for literature circles and reader response, strategies for ESL and interdisciplinary connections, related titles. This 7-page teacher's guide requires Adobe Reader or compatible application for access.

Cool Melons -- Turn to Frogs! The Life and Poems of Issa by Matthew Gollub
This 8-page teacher's guide includes a summary and background information, prereading questions, vocabulary, discussion questions, ideas for literature circles and reader response, strategies for ESL, and interdisciplinary connections. Adobe Reader or compatible application required for access.

Jane Goodall's 10 Ways to Help Save Wildlife by Jane Goodall
Students note details as they read. Includes text-dependent questions, vocabulary words, a culminating task, and additional learning activities. Word processor required for access.

Life on the Ice by Susan E. Goodman
Students note details as they read. Includes text-dependent questions, vocabulary words, a culminating task, and additional learning activities. Word processor required for access.

The Hermit Crab by Carter Goodrich
Summary, analysis, and 5 sets of discussion questions based on events and themes in the story.

Anthony Reynoso: Born to Rope Hardcover by Ginger Gordon
Students practice noting details. Includes text-dependent questions, vocabulary words, a culminating task, and additional learning activities. Word processor required for access.

The Wind in the Willows by Kenneth Grahame
Lesson plans and teaching resources for this children's classic.

Reading and Writing about Miss Moo Goes to the Zoo by Kelly Graves
This lesson provides literature-based reading and writing activities for special education and early elementary students. This lesson may also be used to illustrate the character education traits tolerance, self-respect and compassion.

Rhonda Gowler Greene
Lesson ideas for The Very First Thanksgiving, other books.

A Teacher's Activity Guide for Princess Posey and the First Grade Parade by Stephanie Greene
Discussion questions, graphic organizers, practice with vocabulary, and word study. This 19-page guide requires Adobe Reader for access.
